1.Red Fort when it was built was red and white in color.

2. The design of this fort was made by Ustad Ahmad Lahauri.

3.Red Fort was given the name due to the large red colored walls that surrounds it.

4.The actual name of Red Fort was Qila-e-Mubarak.

5.It is said that it took 10 years to complete the construction of this fort.

6.Red Fort also has a building inside called the Rang Mahal.

7.Red Fort is one place that will be ever remembered for the historic glory.
